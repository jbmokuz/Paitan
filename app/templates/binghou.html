Explination of Binghou!

Just get 5 Squares in a row, horizontally vertically or diagonally

Explination of squares

Fill in a square, you must win a hand with one of the following conditions
  • >Ura2 - Get Ura3 or more!
  • Ikkitsuukan - 123456789
  • >50Fu - have more then 50fu in your winning hand
  • Haneman - get a Haneman
  • Riichi Nomi - Riichi with no other han (so not even uradora)
  • Jikaze - Your wind!
  • Kan! - Just make a kan! (you dont have to win the hand!)
  • 3 Melds - make 3 melds and win the hand
  • Menzen - closed tsumo
  • 3zou win - your winning tile must be the 3zou
  • Other Yaku - get any other yaku not on the board
  • 2 9pin - win a hand with at least 2 9pin
  • All others - Just get that yaku!
